BURMESE PYTHON

Large non-venomous snake known for its size and strength

BURMESE PYTHON FACTS

Physical Traits

Up to 23 feet long
Light and dark patterned skin
Very strong body

Habitat

Southeast Asia
Grasslands and swamps
Also found in water


Diet

Eats birds and mammals
Constrains prey
Slow digestion

One of the longest snakes in the world.
