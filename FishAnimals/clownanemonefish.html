CLOWN ANEMONEFISH

Bright orange reef fish known for living safely among sea anemones

CLOWN ANEMONEFISH FACTS

Physical Traits

Orange body with white stripes
Small rounded fins
About 3–4 inches long

Habitat

Warm, shallow coral reefs
Indo-Pacific region
Lives inside sea anemones for protection


Diet

Algae
Small crustaceans
Plankton and leftover anemone food

MORE ABOUT CLOWN ANEMONEFISHES

Unique Animal

Clownfish and anemones protect each other from predators.

All Born Male

They can change sex—dominant individuals become female.
