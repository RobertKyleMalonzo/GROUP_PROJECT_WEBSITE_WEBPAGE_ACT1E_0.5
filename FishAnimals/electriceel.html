ELECTRIC EEL

A freshwater fish that can deliver powerful electric shocks for hunting and defense

ELECTRIC EEL FACTS

Physical Traits

Long, snake-like body
Dark green or gray
Up to 8 feet long

Habitat

Freshwater rivers and swamps
Amazon & Orinoco basins
Lives in muddy, slow-moving waters


Diet

Fish, crustaceans
Small mammals
Uses electricity to stun prey

MORE ABOUT ELECTRIC EELS

Unique Animal

Can produce up to 600 volts—enough to knock down a human.

Air Breathers

Must surface often because they absorb oxygen through their mouths.
