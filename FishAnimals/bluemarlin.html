BLUE MARLIN

One of the fastest fish in the ocean, known for its long spear-like bill

BLUE MARLIN FACTS

Physical Traits

Long, pointed bill
Cobalt-blue top and silvery belly
Can grow over 14 feet long

Habitat

Tropical & subtropical oceans
Prefers deep offshore waters
Swims in warm surface currents


Diet

Feeds on tuna, mackerel, squid
Uses bill to stun prey
Active predator in open water

MORE ABOUT BLUE MARLINS

Unique Animal

Can swim up to 50–60 mph, making them one of the fastest fish.

Strong Migrators

Travel thousands of miles across oceans each year.
