BULL SHARK

A powerful shark known for living in both saltwater and freshwater

BULL SHARK FACTS

Physical Traits

Stocky, muscular body
Wide, blunt snout
Grey top, white underside

Habitat

Coastal oceans & rivers
Found in warm shallow waters
Can swim far up freshwater rivers


Diet

Fish, rays, dolphins
Eats turtles and smaller sharks
Opportunistic top predator

MORE ABOUT BULL SHARKS

Unique Animal

One of the only sharks that can live fully in freshwater.

Strong and Aggressive

Known for their bold, fearless hunting style.
