ADÉLIE PENGUIN

Playful Antarctic penguin that builds nests with pebbles

ADÉLIE PENGUIN FACTS

Physical Traits

Black and white feathers
Short tail and flippers
Strong swimmers

Habitat

Antarctica
Icy coasts
Lives in large colonies


Diet

Eats krill and fish
Excellent diver
Hunts in groups

MORE ABOUT ADÉLIE PENGUINES

Unique Animal

Builds nests using pebbles and often steals rocks from others.

Ice Explorer

Spends most of its life in freezing ocean waters.
