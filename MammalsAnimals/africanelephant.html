AFRICAN ELEPHANT FACTS

Physical Traits

10–13 feet tall
Long trunk with 40,000 muscles

Habitat

Sub-Saharan Africa
Savannas and forests
Lives in herds


Diet

Up to 300 pounds of plants daily
Eats grass, bark, fruit
Drinks lots of water

MORE ABOUT AFRICAN ELEPHANTS

Unique Animal

Largest land animal on Earth. Uses trunk for breathing, smelling, and grabbing.

Gentle Giant

Can dig burrows 30 feet long. Moves 2 tons of soil when digging.Very intelligent and social. Can recognize themselves in mirrors.
