ARCTIC FOX

Cold-adapted fox from the Arctic with seasonal fur

ARCTIC FOX FACTS

Physical Traits

Small body, thick fur
White coat in winter
Bushy tail for warmth

Habitat

Arctic regions
Tundra and icy areas
Lives in burrows


Diet

Eats rodents and birds
Scavenges leftovers
Stores food for winter

MORE ABOUT ARCTIC FOXES

Unique Animal

Cold Survivor
