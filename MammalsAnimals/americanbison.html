AMERICAN BISON FACTS

Physical Traits

6–7 feet tall
Thick fur and large hump
Sharp curved horns

Habitat

North America
Grasslands and plains
Lives in herds


Diet

Eats grasses and plants
Grazes most of the day
Can survive harsh winters
