CHRISTMAS ISLAND RED CRAB

Bright red land crab famous for its massive annual migration to the ocean

CHRISTMAS ISLAND RED CRAB FACTS

Physical Traits

Bright red shell
Strong climbing legs
4–5 inches wide

Habitat

Christmas Island (Indian Ocean)
Lives in forests and moist burrows
Migrates to coastline for breeding


Diet

Leaves, fruits, flowers
Occasionally scavenges dead animals
Helps recycle forest nutrients

MORE ABOUT CHRISTMAS ISLAND RED CRABS

Unique Animal

Millions migrate yearly to lay eggs at the sea.

Forest Engineers

Their digging shapes the island ecosystem.
