BLACK WIDOW

A venomous spider known for its shiny black body and red hourglass marking

BLACK WIDOW FACTS

Physical Traits

Shiny black body
Red hourglass marking on abdomen
Long, thin legs

Habitat

Warm regions worldwide
Lives in dark sheltered places
Found in woodpiles, sheds, and rocks


Diet

Insects like flies, beetles, mosquitoes
Uses sticky silk webs
Injects venom to digest prey

MORE ABOUT BLACK WIDOWS

Unique Animal

Venom is strong but rarely fatal to humans.

Messy Web Builders

They spin irregular, tangled webs instead of neat circular ones.
