FIREFLY

Glowing beetle that lights up the night using natural bioluminescence

FIREFLY FACTS

Physical Traits

Soft, dark body
Glowing lower abdomen
Long wings

Habitat

Warm, humid environments
Grasslands, forests, marshes
Most active at night


Diet

Some eat insects, snails, or worms
Others drink nectar or pollen
Larvae eat slugs and soft-bodied insects

MORE ABOUT FIREFLYS

Unique Animal

Produces light with no heat using a chemical reaction.

Flash Signals

Each species flashes unique light patterns to communicate.
